Plov

Description

Plov is a popular Central Asian dish made of rice, meat (usually lamb), carrots, and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg lamb or beef
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 onions
  • 2 carrots
  • Salt, pepper, cumin to taste
  • Water for boiling

Steps

  1. Cook the meat until tender.
  2. Add onions and carrots, cooking them until soft.
  3. Stir in rice and spices, and cook until rice is tender.
